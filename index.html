Journey Through A Rustic Konkan Village

Velas Village, Maharashtra

Velas Village is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in Maharashtra. It's home to a number of turtles, villagers and tourist attractions. Turtle festival is a significant part of areas reputation.

Find out more

Check out all the Wildlife

As the hatching season approaches, Olive Ridley turtles are hatched and conserved by the local villagers. The other endangered species found there are vultures, Indian swiftlets, and eagles.

From Tents to Resorts

Velas village is full of wonderful places to stay. You have the ability to sleep in the local houses, or relax and destress yourself at the seashore. Here are our top three places you have to visit:

  • Velas Beach
  • Bantok Fort
  • Harihareshwar beach

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
